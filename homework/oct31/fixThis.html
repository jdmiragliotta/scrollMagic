Symantically speaking there should not be any heading tags within an article tag itself. They should be placed outside the tag. Also, heading tags should not be used for styling purposes, as they are used for creating hierarchy (levels of importance) in your html and are used by search bots to identify the your pages' relation to the said searched topic.
